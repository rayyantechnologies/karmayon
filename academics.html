Kindergaten & Nursery School

The Karmayon kindergaten provides children with a play-based or emergent curriculum, which is child-centred and teacher framed. Our educators develop large and small group instruction as well as learning centres, based on observed student abilities, skills and interests. Age-appropriate instruction, activities will be provided to children daily in: Quran, Arabic, Islamic Practices, Foundational Literacy and Numeracy, and Outdoor Learning. A strong focus is put on developing children’s self-regulation and growing independence within a caring and nurturing environment that considers children’s social, emotional, cognitive, physical and spiritual well being.

Age Requirement:

Parents interested in our preschool must ensure that their child meets the age requirements; children must be at least 2 years old to be eligible to apply. Parents are asked for their child’s proof of age at time of application.

Primary School

Excellence in education for motivated young learners is what our Primary Division strives for with every student. The staff has taken the mission to heart in creating an environment for students to help them become Islamically strong, academically successful, well-balanced, and prepared for future challenges.

Our primary division serves a great number of students from a variety of backgrounds, experiences and cultures. We are committed to supporting each child’s spiritual, social, emotional and intellectual development. Our Islamically infused teaching methods are designed to inspire students to become intellectually inspired and achieve their potential within a stimulating and caring learning environment. The attributes of the Karmayon School serve not only as a guide for the kind of student that we hope will graduate from our school, but for all the learners in our school community: students, teachers and parents. We also hope that parents will encourage the development of these attributes at home in order to support what is being done at school.

We welcome your thoughts, comments, and questions about our elementary program of studies; please do not hesitate to contact us at any time.

Quran School

The London Islamic School believes in connecting our pupils to the most profound text, which formulates the basis of our religion – The Quran. Starting from preschool, our students learn the Quran from the very basics (reading, reciting, and memorization) to the memorization of entire chapters.

Our students learn how to read and recite the Quran with proper tajweed. They are also introduced to the memorization of the last 10 Surahs of the Quran, as well as other passages such as Ayatul Kursi. Students also have the opportunity to learn the meaning and the Arabic Language in our Islamic Studies and Arabic classes.
Our students from primary 1-3 start a thorough memorization process of the Quran, in which they are expected to have memorized the last Juz of the Quran – Juz Amma, by the end of grade 3.

Starting from primary 4, students are placed in smaller class sizes, are given longer blocks of Quran per week, and continue their memorization at their own pace. Each student carries a Quran log, which outlines the memorization goals and achievements for the year. Typically, by the end of SS 3, students memorize five Juz of the Quran, depending on the commitment of the students and parents.
